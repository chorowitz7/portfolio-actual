About Me

My name is Christopher Horowitz and I am a 2020 graduate from Quinnipiac University in Hamden, Connecticut. I have a passion for building programs and responsive applications in the web development and software development field.

My journey started off as a student at the University of Maine in 2016 as a Finance student. After completing a FinTech course during my Spring 2017 semester, I realized that going into the technology field is what I wanted to pursue. Building coupled with technology is the perfect combination to fuel my passion for the web development and software development field.

After my freshman year at the University of Maine, I transferred to Quinnipiac University to pursue my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Information Systems. This change of school and major gave me a great opportunity to do what I am passionate about.

Technology is the now and the future, and I want to be a part of the journey.
